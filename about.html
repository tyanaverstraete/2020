Verstraete Tyana (1999, BE) is a student Ceramic & Glass at Luca School of Arts, Campus Ghent. The most of her works are made out of glass. She starts from a rational idea, which translates into a poetic visual sculpture. Themes that often recur in her work are water and the human body, whether or not in relation to each other. She strives for technical refinement and perfection in every way possible of the visual image.
